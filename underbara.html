UNDERBARA UNGE
- REDESIGN

This is a school project where the goal was to redesign and improve an already existing service, by combining usercentred research with strict requirements for accessibility and sustainability. The mobile app Underbara Unge is made for parents to create a personal memory book of their child's first year.
The final design lowers the threshold for the user with flexible steps and motivational progression. These improvements create a more inclusive and undemanding experience that better supports the user's everyday life and increases the likelihood that the book will be completed.
The new design is more accessibile thanks to the change of color scheme, the new colors reach the WCAG AA standards.
