EQUESTRIAN
MANAGEMENT APP

An idea that arose when I noticed myself keeping up with multiple conversations, bookings and planning accross different platforms. That's why I wanted to create an app that collected everything in one place. Horse owners, stable owners and employees can access and review everything from veterinary appointments to contacting other horse owners in the same stable.
The app is still under construction and currently just an idea, the next step in the work process is to conduct field studies, interviews and investigate what the need actually look like!
